Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-06

Wallet Guardian does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data — ever. Everything runs locally inside your browser.

1 What We Collect

Nothing. The Extension does not collect your name, email, location, browsing history, shopping behavior, or any other personal information. No cookies, no fingerprinting, no tracking of any kind.

2 How It Works

A content script runs locally in your browser tab. It scans the page DOM for purchase-related buttons, and when you hover over one, displays a cat GIF from a locally bundled set of 28 images. No network requests are made. No data leaves your device. All 28 GIF files are packaged inside the Extension at install time — no external resources are ever fetched.

3 Permissions

PermissionWhy it's needed
Host permissions (e-commerce domains) Allows the content script to run on supported shopping sites.
web_accessible_resources Allows the bundled GIF files to be displayed as page overlays.

No permissions to read history, access cookies, or make network requests are requested.
